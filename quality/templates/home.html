Monitor Water Quality

Water from rivers, lakes, and aquifers needs proper monitoring to ensure safety and purity.

Contaminated water spreads diseases like cholera and typhoid. Regular testing ensures safe drinking water for everyone.

Clean water supports health, reduces disease, and improves community well-being.

Everyone deserves clean water.
